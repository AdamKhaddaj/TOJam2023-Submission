Summary

This project introduces a system that evokes the visual style of pencil-drawn imagery by replacing the standard lighting model with a hatching effect that reacts to lighting in real-time. Texturing is done in object-space, meaning that hatching strokes will bend around 3D models to further communicate form and depth.

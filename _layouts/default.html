Summary

Journey on the Stellarix 9000 is our team's submission to the TOJam2023 gamejam event. The theme of the gamejam was "You must leave it behind", and the time limit was 72 hours.

This game is about completing a difficult journey while manipulating the outcome of certain events. You'll need to manage your resources carefully to make it to the end!

Credits to Adam Khaddaj, Hazel Gifford, Daniel Khaddaj, Leo McCarthy Kennedy
